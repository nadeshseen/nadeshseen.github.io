Plant Performance Tracking and Warehouse Management Application

As part of my work at Infosys Ltd. I worked with The Kraft Heinz Company, commonly known as Kraft Heinz, an American food company formed by the merger of Kraft Foods and Heinz. In this project we used Our AI and ML platform NIA for tracking various manufacturing plants of Kraft Heinz located across America and Europe and managing their warehouses. Our application was a web application designed using AngularJs, Java, PostgreSQL. The data was projected to business users in the form of interactive bar graphs and pie charts for better analysis and decision making. My role in the project was a backend developer. I participated in later phases of system development life cycle like project maintenance and analysis. I wrote REST API endpoints in Java and bugfixes in AngularJs.
